Our Story

Thoughtfully Crafted.
Beautifully Presented.

Palm Ave Catering is an elevated event catering company founded by Corrina Hohl, built on the belief that food should be as beautiful as it is delicious. Specializing in high-end finger foods, signature bites, grazing-style displays, dessert cups, and custom beverage bars, Palm Ave Catering brings a polished, creative, and personal touch to every event it serves.

Designed for private parties, baby showers, bridal showers, weddings, corporate gatherings, graduations, and special celebrations, every detail is curated to match your vision — from the first bite to the last sip. While elevated finger foods are the specialty, Corrina is happy to work with you on fully custom menus tailored to your event, guests, and style.
